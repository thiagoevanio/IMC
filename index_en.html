Understanding Body Mass Index (BMI)

BMI is widely used for initial screening. It does not directly measure body fat.

What is Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR)?

BMR estimates the minimum calories needed at rest. For daily needs, use TDEE.

Protein Intake Guide

Protein supports muscle maintenance and satiety. Total daily intake matters most.

FAQ

Are these calculators accurate?

They are estimates based on widely used formulas. Accuracy varies by individual and measurement quality.

What is the difference between BMR and TDEE?

BMR estimates calories at rest. TDEE estimates daily calories by multiplying BMR by an activity factor.

Is this medical advice?

No. These tools are educational and do not replace professional medical guidance.
