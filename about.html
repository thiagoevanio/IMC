About IMCEPROTEINA

Simplifying nutrition with technology.

Our Mission

We believe that access to basic health information should be free, fast, and easy to understand. We created IMCEPROTEINA.COM.BR to eliminate the complexity of nutritional mathematical formulas.

Our tool unifies three of the most important calculations for those seeking quality of life: Body Mass Index (BMI), Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), and Protein Intake.

Our Values

  • Precision: We use globally recognized formulas (WHO, Mifflin-St Jeor).
  • Privacy: We do not store your data.
  • Simplicity: Clean and direct design.
